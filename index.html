Our Legacy

Founded in 1985, Shubh has been serving authentic regional cuisine for over three decades. Our journey began with a simple vision: to preserve and present the rich culinary heritage of India while embracing modern gastronomy.

Today, we stand as a testament to traditional cooking methods merged with contemporary dining experiences. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, each carrying a story of its own.
